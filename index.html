Interesting Facts about teddy-bear

  • The teddy was named after President Theodore Roosevelt, after he refused to shoot a bear during a hunting trip.
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  • The incident of Roosevelt sparing the bear led to the popularity and naming of Teddy bears

Interesting Facts about panda

  • They have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food.
  • Like domestic cats, giant pandas have vertical slits for pupils.
  • Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo.

Interesting Facts about flying cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus
  • They improve lawns by digging tunnels that allow air into the soil. When they die, their rotting bodies put nitrogen into the soil.
